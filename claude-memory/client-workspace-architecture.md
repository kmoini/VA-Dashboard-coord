Consensus reached 2026-06-12 on the **client workspace** (a.k.a. client portal). Clients and accountants share ONE login: `my.voiceaccountant.com/login` (single `GET /login`, `guest` mw). After auth the app branches on `users.type`: `accountant`→`/dashboard`, `client`→`/client/dashboard` (already enforced in [ResolveTenant.php:82]).

**Ownership reframe:** the Client is the PRIMARY entity owning a personal workspace; the accountant *connects to* it (like the existing mobile↔dashboard relationship), rather than the firm owning the client. Every client gets a **personal workspace = a personal firm (`AccountantAccount`) + their own `Client` record** — reuses 100% of firm-scoping/quota/audit. Self-serve clients can log into the dashboard with NO accountant invite (use case: upload tx files from home PC instead of mobile).

**Connection lifecycle:** accountant connection = the union-plan relationship (`user_accountant` mobile-side source-of-truth, mirrored via [AccountantInboxController], `clients.mobile_user_id`). Client can find/change/disconnect their accountant FROM the dashboard, routed through `MobileRelationshipService` (one source of truth → needs a union-plan ADR with Mobile Claude). On connect, the client record is **re-parented** into the accountant's firm (today `accept` CREATES a new Client — must change to re-parent/merge the existing personal client, not duplicate). Disconnect returns it to the personal workspace.

**Client write surface = read-only on the ledger + exactly 3 writes:** (1) loose document upload single+batch → triage inbox as un-linked attachments (`attachable_id` null), `source=client`; (2) attach a file to one of their own transactions; (3) append-only NOTE on a transaction (separate annotation, NOT an edit of the accountant's `comment` or any accounting field). All audit-logged, quota-checked. Records view = ALL their own transactions (not approved-only). CLAUDE.md "strictly read-only" wording to be updated to "read-only on the ledger; may upload documents + add notes."

**Billing:** NOT free. Self-serve/unconnected client pays their OWN Stripe subscription (billing gate applies; client-facing checkout). Connected-to-accountant client is covered by the accountant's plan (no separate charge). Client billing section shows App Store + Google Play links (one product unlocks dashboard+mobile). Open: Stripe-on-dashboard vs app-store IAP reconciliation → union-plan ADR.
